Meat Pie

Description

A meat pie is a pie with a filling of meat and often with other savory ingredients. They are found in cuisines worldwide. However, we will be making an african-style meat pie today.

Ingredients

  • Meat: The "meat" in this meat pie is a combination of ground beef and ground pork.
  • Spices and seasoning: This hearty meat pie is flavored with garlic, mustard powder, dried thyme, dried sage, ground cloves, salt, and pepper.
  • vegetables: You'll need a potato and an onion. You can add other veges if you want.
  • Pie Crust: Use a store-bought or homemade double pastry crust.

Steps

  1. Boil and mash the potatoes.
  2. Cook the meat with the water, onion, garlic, and seasonings.
  3. Mix in the potatoes, then fill the pie crust. Cover with the top crust.
  4. Bake until the crust is browned.
