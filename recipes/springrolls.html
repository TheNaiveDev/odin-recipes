Spring Rolls

Description

Spring rolls are rolled appetizers or dim sum commonly found in Chinese and other Southeast Asian cuisines. The kind of wrapper, fillings, and cooking technique used, as well as the name, vary considerably within this large area, depending on the region's culture. Source : wikipedia

Ingredients Needed

  • Floor
  • Mixed vegetables
  • vegetable oil
  • Some margarine
  • Chopped chicken breasts
  • Egg white
  • Some salt
  • Stock cube
  • gravy powder

Steps

  1. Pour the flour into a mixing bowl and some salt to taste
  2. Add some margarine and a few drops of vegetable oil and mix them together
  3. Add some water and mix it to make it into soft dough
  4. Put your saucepan on the fire and add about 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil.
  5. Add some onions and fry for one minute
  6. Add the chicken breasts and allow it to cook for about 4 minutes and add some water to make it saucy
  7. Add some gravy powder to thicken the sauce
  8. Once done, take it off the fire and make it cool
  9. Get a chopping board and a rolling pin and then roll the doll till it is flat and cut it into preferred sizes
  10. Using the egg white as a sticky medium, brush the dough with the egg white
  11. Using a spoon, fill each dough with some of the sauce you made before and fold them nicely. Make sure ends are tucked in properly.
  12. Put a reasonable amount of oil into a sauce pan and put it on fire.
  13. Wait till the oil is very hot and then put the spring rolls in it.
  14. Wait till the rolls turn golden brown and dish them out, voila you have your spring rolls!
